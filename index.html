About Me

I've been practicing coding from a young age, learning DOS-CLI at the age of five. My first foray into programming was designing hosted webspaces utilizing tools offered by the Neopets service, and from there it was a steady expansion into other projects, other languages. Today, I continue to expound on this passion, and keeping these skills current in an ever-shifting workspace!

I've been fortunate enough to join the CommonWealth Coders program, and experience the services offered through their affiliation with Career TEAM. Completion of this course will formally certify the skills I've developed for the working world.
